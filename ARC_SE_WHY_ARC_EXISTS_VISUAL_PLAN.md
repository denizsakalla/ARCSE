# ARC SE - Why ARC SE Exists Visual Plan

## Goal

The video should feel like a documentary introduction to a category that was discovered under pressure.

It should not feel like:

- a trading bot ad
- a crypto promo
- a pitch deck animation
- a generic technology commercial

## Visual Language

Use the website's current language:

- white field
- deep ink typography
- restrained blue accent
- real screenshots
- quiet motion
- no stock imagery
- no neon terminal
- no fake 3D
- no generic "AI" imagery

Reference feel: Gemini x Apple x Anthropic x Stripe.

## Editing Rules

1. The first minute is mostly typography.
2. Screenshots only appear when the script reaches proof.
3. No chart should appear before the viewer understands why forgetting matters.
4. Evidence appears as documents and screenshots, not decoration.
5. The final question gets silence and space.

## Motion Rules

- Fade through white.
- Slow push on screenshots.
- No glitch transitions.
- No fast zooms.
- No crypto-style market flashes.
- Use cuts for the brutal cost lines.
- Use slow dissolves for discovery and proof.

## Color Rules

- Background: #f8fbff or white.
- Main type: #07142d.
- Accent: #3f73ff.
- Evidence cards: white with light blue border.
- Do not use dark HUD colors except inside real screenshots that already contain them.

## Typography

Use available system fonts:

- Headline: Georgia or Times New Roman.
- Interface text: Segoe UI or Inter-like system sans.
- Subtitles: restrained Segoe UI captions in muted ink, burned into the silent review MP4 for timing verification.

## Scene Construction

### Opening

No dashboard.
No chart.
No logo animation.

Just:

People leave.
Teams dissolve.
Systems change.
Knowledge disappears.

### Cost

Use three blunt text frames.

No metaphor.
No illustration.

### Experiment

Can operational intelligence survive its creators?
